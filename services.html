The Story

On each project, Reid sets out to take the best photographs possible, working hard to make all shoots as close to that perfect vision as possible.
In doing so Datsuns-R-Us has obtained a worldwide following on social media with more than 1.25 million fans on his verified Facebook page to date.

The Cars

For Reid the cars are about showing the beauty of every datsun but with a custom flare that matches the owners personality. Growing up Reid had always seen modified and classic Datsuns and admired their raw beauty and it wasn't until summer of 2012 when he was able to take a 1976 Datsun 260Z for a spin that he truely fell in love and started the search for his very own. Now having found one he wants to capture and share the beauty of his own and others Datsuns.

What we offer

Premium Photoshoot: ($150) includes a custom digital album fully edited and composed into a slideshow as well as raw high res copies.

Touch-Ups: (prices vary) do you have some cool photos of your ride but need to add some custom flare, look no further we can offer you premium editing services on all your photos.
